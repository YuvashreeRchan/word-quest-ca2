Instructions

Hello,!

Welcome to the Word Quest Game!

To play the game, follow these instructions:

  1. Enter a letter into the input field provided one by one.
  2. Click the "Submit" button to submit your guess.
  3. You have a limited number of guesses to uncover the hidden word.
  4. If your guess is correct, the letter will be revealed in its correct position(s) in the word.
  5. If your guess is incorrect, you will lose a guess.
  6. Keep guessing until you uncover the entire word within 60 seconds or run out of guesses.
  7. There are total of 5 rounds to increase your scores.
  8. Good luck!
